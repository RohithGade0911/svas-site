Dal Bafla: common questions

How many calories are in Dal Bafla?

One serving of Dal Bafla (200g) has 320 calories. That works out to 160 calories per 100g.

How much protein is in Dal Bafla?

A serving of Dal Bafla has 10.3g of protein, about 13% of its calories.

Is Dal Bafla vegetarian?

Yes. Dal Bafla is a vegetarian dish.

What is Dal Bafla made of?

Dal Bafla is made with Whole wheat flour, Red gram dal, Ghee and Bengal gram dal.
