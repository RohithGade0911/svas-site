Puliyodarai: common questions

How many calories are in Puliyodarai?

One serving of Puliyodarai (200g) has 380 calories. That works out to 190 calories per 100g.

How much protein is in Puliyodarai?

A serving of Puliyodarai has 8.6g of protein, about 9% of its calories.

Is Puliyodarai vegetarian?

Yes. Puliyodarai is a vegetarian dish.

What is Puliyodarai made of?

Puliyodarai is made with Rice, Tamarind, pulp, Groundnut and Groundnut oil.
