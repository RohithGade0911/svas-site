Puran Poli: common questions

How many calories are in Puran Poli?

One serving of Puran Poli (125g) has 354 calories. That works out to 283 calories per 100g.

How much protein is in Puran Poli?

A serving of Puran Poli has 9.7g of protein, about 11% of its calories.

Is Puran Poli vegetarian?

Yes. Puran Poli is a vegetarian dish.

What is Puran Poli made of?

Puran Poli is made with Bengal gram dal, Jaggery, cane, Whole wheat flour and Ghee.

What are the macros in Puran Poli?
