Madhya Pradesh food: common questions

What are the most popular dishes from Madhya Pradesh?

Well-known Madhya Pradesh dishes include Bhopali Keema, Dal Bafla, Palak Puri and Poha Jalebi. You can see the full per-serving nutrition for each on its own page.

How many calories are in Madhya Pradesh food?

Across the Madhya Pradesh dishes in our dataset, a typical serving averages 223 calories and 7.8g of protein. Individual dishes vary, so each one has its own breakdown.

Where does the nutrition data come from?

Every value is computed from IFCT-2017, the Indian Food Composition Tables from the National Institute of Nutrition, as the per-serving sum of each ingredient, and reviewed by a dietitian.
