Tamil Nadu food: common questions

What are the most popular dishes from Tamil Nadu?

Well-known Tamil Nadu dishes include Dindigul Mutton Biryani, Chicken Chettinad, Meen Kuzhambu and Medu Vada. You can see the full per-serving nutrition for each on its own page.

How many calories are in Tamil Nadu food?

Across the Tamil Nadu dishes in our dataset, a typical serving averages 277 calories and 10.6g of protein. Individual dishes vary, so each one has its own breakdown.

Where does the nutrition data come from?

Every value is computed from IFCT-2017, the Indian Food Composition Tables from the National Institute of Nutrition, as the per-serving sum of each ingredient, and reviewed by a dietitian.
