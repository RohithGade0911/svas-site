Tripura food: common questions

What are the most popular dishes from Tripura?

Well-known Tripura dishes include Berma Fish Curry, Wahan Mosdeng, Awan Bangwi and Gudok. You can see the full per-serving nutrition for each on its own page.

How many calories are in Tripura food?

Across the Tripura dishes in our dataset, a typical serving averages 97 calories and 8.7g of protein. Individual dishes vary, so each one has its own breakdown.

Where does the nutrition data come from?

Every value is computed from IFCT-2017, the Indian Food Composition Tables from the National Institute of Nutrition, as the per-serving sum of each ingredient, and reviewed by a dietitian.
