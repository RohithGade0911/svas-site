West Bengal food: common questions

What are the most popular dishes from West Bengal?

Well-known West Bengal dishes include Kosha Mangsho, Doi Maach, Macher Jhol and Chingri Malaikari. You can see the full per-serving nutrition for each on its own page.

How many calories are in West Bengal food?

Across the West Bengal dishes in our dataset, a typical serving averages 194 calories and 10.8g of protein. Individual dishes vary, so each one has its own breakdown.

Where does the nutrition data come from?

Every value is computed from IFCT-2017, the Indian Food Composition Tables from the National Institute of Nutrition, as the per-serving sum of each ingredient, and reviewed by a dietitian.
