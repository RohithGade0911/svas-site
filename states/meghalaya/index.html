Meghalaya food: common questions

What are the most popular dishes from Meghalaya?

Well-known Meghalaya dishes include Dohneiiong, Jadoh, Dohkhleh and Putharo. You can see the full per-serving nutrition for each on its own page.

How many calories are in Meghalaya food?

Across the Meghalaya dishes in our dataset, a typical serving averages 203 calories and 12.2g of protein. Individual dishes vary, so each one has its own breakdown.

Where does the nutrition data come from?

Every value is computed from IFCT-2017, the Indian Food Composition Tables from the National Institute of Nutrition, as the per-serving sum of each ingredient, and reviewed by a dietitian.
