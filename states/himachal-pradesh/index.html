Himachal Pradesh food: common questions

What are the most popular dishes from Himachal Pradesh?

Well-known Himachal Pradesh dishes include Pahari Chicken Curry, Mutton Palda, Chana Madra and Rajma Madra. You can see the full per-serving nutrition for each on its own page.

How many calories are in Himachal Pradesh food?

Across the Himachal Pradesh dishes in our dataset, a typical serving averages 234 calories and 13.5g of protein. Individual dishes vary, so each one has its own breakdown.

Where does the nutrition data come from?

Every value is computed from IFCT-2017, the Indian Food Composition Tables from the National Institute of Nutrition, as the per-serving sum of each ingredient, and reviewed by a dietitian.
