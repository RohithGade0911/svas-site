Punjab food: common questions

What are the most popular dishes from Punjab?

Well-known Punjab dishes include Tandoori Chicken, Butter Chicken, Chicken Tikka and Amritsari Fish. You can see the full per-serving nutrition for each on its own page.

How many calories are in Punjab food?

Across the Punjab dishes in our dataset, a typical serving averages 260 calories and 13.2g of protein. Individual dishes vary, so each one has its own breakdown.

Where does the nutrition data come from?

Every value is computed from IFCT-2017, the Indian Food Composition Tables from the National Institute of Nutrition, as the per-serving sum of each ingredient, and reviewed by a dietitian.
