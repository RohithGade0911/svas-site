Rajasthan food: common questions

What are the most popular dishes from Rajasthan?

Well-known Rajasthan dishes include Safed Maas, Laal Maas, Dal Baati Churma and Gatte ki Sabzi. You can see the full per-serving nutrition for each on its own page.

How many calories are in Rajasthan food?

Across the Rajasthan dishes in our dataset, a typical serving averages 251 calories and 11.3g of protein. Individual dishes vary, so each one has its own breakdown.

Where does the nutrition data come from?

Every value is computed from IFCT-2017, the Indian Food Composition Tables from the National Institute of Nutrition, as the per-serving sum of each ingredient, and reviewed by a dietitian.
