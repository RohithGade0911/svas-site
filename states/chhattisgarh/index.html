Chhattisgarh food: common questions

What are the most popular dishes from Chhattisgarh?

Well-known Chhattisgarh dishes include Bara, Baingan Bharta, Til Laddu and Chila. You can see the full per-serving nutrition for each on its own page.

How many calories are in Chhattisgarh food?

Across the Chhattisgarh dishes in our dataset, a typical serving averages 195 calories and 5.6g of protein. Individual dishes vary, so each one has its own breakdown.

Where does the nutrition data come from?

Every value is computed from IFCT-2017, the Indian Food Composition Tables from the National Institute of Nutrition, as the per-serving sum of each ingredient, and reviewed by a dietitian.
