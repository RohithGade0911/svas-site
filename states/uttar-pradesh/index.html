Uttar Pradesh food: common questions

What are the most popular dishes from Uttar Pradesh?

Well-known Uttar Pradesh dishes include Awadhi Chicken Korma, Mutton Nihari, Shami Kebab and Galouti Kebab. You can see the full per-serving nutrition for each on its own page.

How many calories are in Uttar Pradesh food?

Across the Uttar Pradesh dishes in our dataset, a typical serving averages 285 calories and 12.6g of protein. Individual dishes vary, so each one has its own breakdown.

Where does the nutrition data come from?

Every value is computed from IFCT-2017, the Indian Food Composition Tables from the National Institute of Nutrition, as the per-serving sum of each ingredient, and reviewed by a dietitian.
