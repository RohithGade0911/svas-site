Andhra Pradesh food: common questions

What are the most popular dishes from Andhra Pradesh?

Well-known Andhra Pradesh dishes include Chicken 65, Andhra Chicken Biryani, Gongura Mutton and Kodi Kura. You can see the full per-serving nutrition for each on its own page.

How many calories are in Andhra Pradesh food?

Across the Andhra Pradesh dishes in our dataset, a typical serving averages 343 calories and 15.4g of protein. Individual dishes vary, so each one has its own breakdown.

Where does the nutrition data come from?

Every value is computed from IFCT-2017, the Indian Food Composition Tables from the National Institute of Nutrition, as the per-serving sum of each ingredient, and reviewed by a dietitian.
