Bihar food: common questions

What are the most popular dishes from Bihar?

Well-known Bihar dishes include Bihari Kabab, Champaran Mutton, Sattu Paratha and Litti Chokha. You can see the full per-serving nutrition for each on its own page.

How many calories are in Bihar food?

Across the Bihar dishes in our dataset, a typical serving averages 238 calories and 11.7g of protein. Individual dishes vary, so each one has its own breakdown.

Where does the nutrition data come from?

Every value is computed from IFCT-2017, the Indian Food Composition Tables from the National Institute of Nutrition, as the per-serving sum of each ingredient, and reviewed by a dietitian.
