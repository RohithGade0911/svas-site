Karnataka food: common questions

What are the most popular dishes from Karnataka?

Well-known Karnataka dishes include Kori Gassi, Vangi Bhath, Bisi Bele Bath and Chitranna. You can see the full per-serving nutrition for each on its own page.

How many calories are in Karnataka food?

Across the Karnataka dishes in our dataset, a typical serving averages 301 calories and 8.1g of protein. Individual dishes vary, so each one has its own breakdown.

Where does the nutrition data come from?

Every value is computed from IFCT-2017, the Indian Food Composition Tables from the National Institute of Nutrition, as the per-serving sum of each ingredient, and reviewed by a dietitian.
