Svas vs HealthifyMe: which one fits how you eat?

Both apps help you eat better in India, but they are built for different jobs. Here is a straight comparison so you can pick the right one, not just the bigger one.

HealthifyMe is the best-known health app in India, with more than 40 million users, an AI assistant, human coaches and device integrations. It does a lot. Svas does one thing: it tells you the real macros in regional Indian food and plans your week around the dishes you already eat.

If you want a coach, workout plans and photo-based logging in a single subscription, HealthifyMe is the broader product and we will say so plainly below. If what you actually want is trustworthy numbers for a katori of dal, a plate of pesarattu or your grandmother's curry, that is exactly the gap Svas was built to close.

The honest difference is not features. It is where the nutrition numbers come from.

Where HealthifyMe is the better choice

We are not going to pretend otherwise. HealthifyMe is the stronger pick if you want a human coach checking in on you, AI photo logging for quick entries, workout and weight-loss programmes, or syncing with a smartwatch and a CGM. It is a mature platform with a huge user base, and for many people that breadth is the point.

Where Svas is the better choice

Svas wins when accuracy and regional depth matter more than breadth. If you have ever logged "Indian curry" and felt the number was a guess, that is the problem we set out to fix.

  • You eat regional, home-style food. Pesarattu, puttu, macher jhol, undhiyu, bisi bele bath. Each is a real dish in Svas, not a generic stand-in.
  • You want numbers you can trust. Every macro is the per-serving sum of each ingredient's IFCT-2017 value, then dietitian-checked. If a value is not verified, it does not ship.
  • You want simple, not a coaching funnel. One flat plan, a weekly plan built around your goal, and no pressure to upgrade into expensive tiers.

Pick HealthifyMe if

  • You want a coach or AI chat
  • You log mostly by photo or barcode
  • You want workouts and device syncing in one app
  • You are focused on a structured weight-loss programme

Pick Svas if

  • You eat regional Indian food every day
  • You care that the macros are actually correct

  • You want one honest price, no upsell

The bottom line

HealthifyMe is a platform. Svas is a specialist. If you want the widest set of features and coaching, go with HealthifyMe. If you want the most accurate, most regional view of the Indian food you actually eat, that is the whole reason Svas exists.

Common questions

Is Svas a HealthifyMe alternative?

Svas is an alternative for one specific job: knowing the real macros in regional Indian food and planning a week around it. HealthifyMe is a broader fitness platform with coaching, AI photo logging and device integrations. If your main need is accurate Indian-food nutrition without a coach, Svas is the focused option.

How is the nutrition data different?

Svas computes every dish from IFCT-2017, the Indian Food Composition Tables published by the National Institute of Nutrition, as the per-serving sum of each ingredient, and a dietitian reviews it. Large crowd databases rely on user-submitted entries, which is why the same dish can show several different calorie values.

Which app is cheaper?

Svas is a flat plan at a low monthly or yearly price with no coaching upsell. HealthifyMe ranges from a budget self-serve plan up to premium coaching and CGM plans that cost several thousand rupees a month.

Does Svas have an AI coach?

No. Svas is built around a verified food database and a rule-based weekly planner, not an AI chatbot or a human coach. If you specifically want coaching or AI photo logging, HealthifyMe covers that.
