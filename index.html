Questions, answered.

Is there a nutrition app that understands Indian food?

Yes, that's why we built it. Most apps assume Western food and leave you guessing at a katori of dal. Svas is built around Indian food, so you can log what you actually eat.

Which regional cuisines does Svas cover?

Dishes from 28 states, from pesarattu to rajma chawal to macher jhol. Each one shown in its own script, because your food deserves its real name.

Where does Svas get its nutrition data?

From India's own food data, checked by dietitians (source: nin.res.in). We don't guess or scrape numbers off the internet. If a value isn't verified, it doesn't ship.

Can Svas track macros and calories for home-cooked meals?

Yes. Svas knows the ingredients and portions behind everyday cooking, so you get real per-serving numbers for the meals you make, not a generic "Indian curry" guess.

Does Svas help with weight loss or muscle gain?

Svas builds your week around your goal, whether that's losing weight, gaining muscle, or just eating better, using food you already love. It also works around conditions like diabetes, PCOS, blood pressure and thyroid.

Does Svas have an AI nutritionist?

Yes, SvasAI. Ask it anything, get a regional meal idea, or log a meal just by chatting. It speaks your language, and its answers come from your real plan, never made-up numbers.

What can Smart Scan do?

Snap a photo of your plate and SvasAI names the food and logs it for you. The fastest way to track a home-cooked meal. (Premium.)

How much does Svas cost?

The core app is free forever. Pro is ₹149/month or ₹999/year and adds the full SvasAI chat. Premium is ₹249/month or ₹1,799/year and adds Smart Scan and more. Prices in INR, taxes as applicable.
